Instructions

Feeling Creative? Here's how you can make Grime Music!

Choose a set of sounds to load up from soundsets 1 to 3.

If you are using a phone or tablet, you can tap the pads to make a sound.

If you are using a keyboard, you can tap the following keys to play the sounds:

Soundset 1 = 1, 2, 3, 4

Soundset 2 = 5, 6, 7, 8

Soundset 3 = 9, 0 , -, =

Once you have made a melody you like, choose a loop from the audio player below the pads to let your creations come to life!
